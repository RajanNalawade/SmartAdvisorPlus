Introduction:

 

SBI Life - Smart Wealth Assure is an Individual, Unit Linked, Non Participating, Life Insurance Product. It is a single premium plan, wherein you have to pay premiums once and you can continue to enjoy the benefits throughout the policy term.

 

The plan helps you to enjoy market related returns along with insurance cover, with just a single premium.

 

Key Features:

 

  • Protection: Financial support for the family, in case of any unfortunate insured event
  • Option to choose a mix of funds providing Market Linked Returns
  • Market Linked Returns provided through 7 funds - Bond Fund, Equity Fund, Balanced Fund, Bond Optimiser Fund, Corporate Bond Fund, Money Market Fund & Pure Fund
  • Pay only once and get the benefits throughout the Policy Term
  • Liquidity through Partial Withdrawal(s) after 5th policy anniversary
  • Option to enhance the protection with Accidental Death Benefit Option.
