Benefits: 

 

^Guaranteed Additions:

 

Guaranteed Additions would apply on the cumulative premiums paid, which is the sum of the premiums paid by the policyholder till date, excluding the applicable taxes, underwriting extra premiums and loading for the modal premium, if any, at the end of each policy year for in-force policies, at a simple rate. The percentage of Guaranteed Addition will be based on the annualized# premium selected. 

These guaranteed additions are payable on maturity of the policy along with Basic Sum Assured or on earlier death of the life assured along with Sum Assured on Death.

 

#Annualized Premium shall be the premium amount payable in a year, chosen by the policyholder, excluding applicable taxes, underwriting extra premiums and loadings for modal premiums, if any.

 

The Guaranteed Additions will be 5.5%** for Annualized premiums less than Rs. 30,000 and 6.0%** for Annualized premiums greater than or equal to Rs. 30,000.

 

** The Guaranteed Additions defined above is at a simple rate and not at compounding rates.
