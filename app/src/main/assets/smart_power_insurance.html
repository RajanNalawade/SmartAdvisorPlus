Introduction: 

To have a secure future, which takes care of the financial needs of the family in case of any eventuality and to meet life's various goals, it is important that one starts saving early and regularly.

We present to you, SBI Life - Smart Power Insurance, an Individual, Unit-linked, Non-Participating Life Insurance Product which takes care of your insurance as well as investment needs. The product offers an affordable premium and is in tune with the current investment scenario. It is a flexible product, which also takes care of your liquidity needs. Additional protection requirement, due to changing personal and/or financial circumstances, is also taken care by the product.

Benefits:
   

●

Maturity Benefit: On survival of the life assured up to maturity, the Fund Value shall be paid in lump sum.

●

Death Benefit: In the unfortunate event of death of the Life assured, while the policy is in-force, Higher of A, B & C is payable to nominee/beneficiary/legal Heir:

  1. Fund Value as on the date of intimation of death  
  2. Basic Sum Assured, as applicable,  less Applicable Partial Withdrawals (APW)#, if any 
  3. 105% of the total Premiums received upto the date of death less Applicable Partial Withdrawals (APW)#, if any

 

#APW is equal to partial withdrawals, if any, in the last 2 years immediately preceding the death of the Life Assured. 
Settlement Option: Nominee or beneficiary or legal heir has the option to receive the Death Benefit, in instalments over 2 to 5 years under 'Settlement' Option as yearly, half-yearly, quarterly or monthly payouts as required, from the date of death.

Note: During the settlement period, the investment risk in the investment portfolio is borne by the beneficiary.

●

In-built Accelerated Total and Permanent Disability (TPD) Benefit: In case of TPD due to Accident or Sickness, 100% of the Death Benefit (as defined above) will be paid immediately and thereafter the policy would terminate.
