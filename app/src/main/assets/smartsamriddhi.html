Benefits: 

^Guaranteed Additions:

Guaranteed Additions are added at a simple rate on the sum of the annualized# premiums paid , at the end of each policy year. The percentage of Guaranteed Addition will be based on the annualized# premium selected. 

#Annualized Premium  shall  be   the  premium  amount   payable  in  a   year,  chosen  by  the policyholder, excluding applicable taxes, underwriting extra premiums and loadings for modal premiums, if any.

The Guaranteed Additions are defined as percentage of the sum of annualized# premiums paid by the policyholder till date,  at the end of  each policy year, for in-force policies. It will be 5.5%** for Annualized premiums ranging from  Rs. 12,000 to Rs. 29,000 and 6.0%* * for Annualized premiums ranging from  Rs. 30,000 to Rs. 75,000

* * The Guaranteed Additions defined above is at a simple rate and not at compounding rates.
