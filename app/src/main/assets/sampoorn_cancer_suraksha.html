▶How does the Benefit structure work?

This product is a simple yet comprehensive cancer care product. You can purchase this product on answering a basic medical questionnaire. You have 3 benefit structures to choose from and the benefits offered under each benefit structure is as below:

  • Standard Option - Covers Minor & Major stage cancer
  • Classic Option - Covers Minor & Major stage cancer, and additional payout on Advanced stage cancer
  • Enhanced Option - Covers Minor & Major stage cancer with additional payout on Advanced stage cancer, and with benefit of Sum  assured reset   

The payouts for this product are designed in way to provide continuous financial assistance.
